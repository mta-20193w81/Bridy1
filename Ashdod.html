Galia Lahav

Over the past three decades, Galia Lahav has established itself as one of the leading couture fashion houses, with garments characterized by their high-end fabrications, meticulous sewing techniques, and of course, our unique technology which enables us to make each garment meet the wearer’s exact measurements from overseas. These values have been instilled in our fashion house for several generations and have resulted in our worldwide popularity. Today, we are proud to have over 70 retailers worldwide and to be able to take part in life’s most significant moments for our brides, red carpet stars and all those fascinated by the art of fashion’s craft.

Address: Haorgim St 20, Ashdod

Berta

Berta has an avant-gard design style, one that always tests the barriers and boundaries of the modern world of fashion design. Her style of design is famous for bold, sexy and modern fit that incorporates vintage materials and retro styles. Aside from designing all the collections at the fashion house, Berta oversees the entire production processes in her studio still to this day, when she has over 150 retailers all around the world.

Address: Haorgim St 25, Ashdod

Riki Dalal

Riki Dalal is a Haute Couture bridal gown designer producing extravagant gowns for every bride’s style. Providing brides in over 15 countries with bridal and evening dresses does not stop Riki Dalal from making sure every dress is of top-notch quality. Riki is also a grandmother of four. Riki can be described as modest and shy even with all the publicity surrounding her designs. She loves nothing more than to be surrounded by her family and to provide her brides with the opportunity to make their wedding dress dreams come true.

Address: Ha-Gdud ha-Ivri St 6, Ashdod

Natali Sisauri

Natalie Sisauri has been a leading fashion designer for more than 25 years, exhibiting great talent and endless creativity, placing her on an international level. Natalie, who has a bridal salon in Ashdod, is constantly upgrading her professional standards and is making sure she adapts to the latest fashion. Every year she presents a dazzling new collection of wedding and evening dresses, from which every bride can find the dress of her dreams. All of Natalie's dresses are made of high-quality and prestigious fabrics, meticulously sewn, with style and luxury.

Address: Haorgim St 18, Ashdod
